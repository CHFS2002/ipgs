Working Groups

Working Group Leadership

Each of the seven thematic Working Groups is led by an elected Head. Heads participate in the Board in a non-voting capacity unless delegated. All positions will be constituted at the Founding Meeting.

WG 1

Grafting Biology

Molecular interactions at the graft union, epigenetic reprogramming, and systems biology.

Head: To be elected

WG 2

Climate-Adaptive Grafting

Drought, heat, salinity, and waterlogging tolerance through strategic rootstock selection.

Head: To be elected

WG 3

Soil Health & Biotic Stress

Grafting-mediated resistance to soilborne pathogens and nematodes.

Head: To be elected

WG 4

Yield, Quality & Nutrition

Effects on yield, flavor, nutritional composition, and post-harvest performance.

Head: To be elected

WG 5

Mechanization & AI

Robotic grafting, machine vision, and AI-assisted compatibility matching.

Head: To be elected

WG 6

Standardization & Protocols

Harmonized protocols and the MIGE standard for reproducible experimentation.

Head: To be elected

WG 7

Capacity Building & Technology Transfer

Training programs, curriculum, fellowships, and technology transfer.

Head: To be elected

Advisory

Scientific Advisory Committee

The SAC provides independent scientific oversight and peer review of Flagship proposals, authors the annual State of Grafting Science, and reviews SOPs before publication. Members are senior scientists drawn from outside the founding cohort, covering all seven thematic areas. Composition will be confirmed following the Founding Meeting.
