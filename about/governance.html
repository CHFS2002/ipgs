Governance Architecture

IPGS is governed through a lean, regionally representative structure designed to be academically appropriate to a research society — not to mimic a corporate organization.

The General Assembly

  • Composition: all Founding, Full, and Associate Members; Observers attend without vote.
  • Convening: annually, in person or hybrid, usually alongside the biennial Symposium.
  • Responsibilities: ratification of the Strategic Plan; admission of new Members; election of Regional Directors; approval of the annual budget; amendment of the Charter.

The Board of Directors

  • Chair (President) · 1 seat · 4-year term: elected by the General Assembly; chairs the Board and represents the Society externally.
  • Co-Chairs / Regional Directors · 6 seats · 4-year term: one per region (Asia-Pacific, East Asia, Europe, Americas, Africa/MENA, Oceania); elected by their regional members.
  • General Secretary · 1 seat · 4-year term: based at HZAU-CHFS (ex officio); oversees the Secretariat and Knowledge Commons.
  • Secretaries · 10 seats: 4 at the Secretariat and one per region, supporting each Co-Chair and coordinating regional activities.
  • Heads of Working Groups · 7 seats: one per thematic WG, elected by WG members; non-voting on Board resolutions unless delegated.
  • Cadence: quarterly virtual meetings; one in-person session annually.

The Scientific Advisory Council (SAC)

  • Size: 9–12 members, nominated by the Board and confirmed by the Assembly.
  • Composition: senior scientists across the seven thematic areas, plus at least one early-career researcher.
  • Responsibilities: peer review of Flagship proposals; authorship of the Annual State of Grafting Science; review of SOPs before publication.

The Secretariat

  • Host: College of Horticulture & Forestry Sciences, HZAU.
  • Functions: administration; meetings coordination; website and Knowledge Commons operations; Charter custodianship; budget.
  • Staffing: Secretary-General (ex officio Board member), Scientific Program Officer, Communications Officer, Data Steward.
