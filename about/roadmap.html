Establishment Roadmap

From founding to global recognition — a phased, milestone-driven approach spanning 2026–2030+.

Phase 0 · 2026 Q1–Q2

Founding

  • Recruit 5–8 anchor institutions.
  • Draft and legally review the Alliance Charter.
  • Appoint an interim Board at HZAU.
Phase 1 · 2026 Q3–Q4

Launch

  • Sign the Charter and MOUs with Founding Members at the Wuhan Founding Meeting (7–10 May 2026).
  • Establish the Secretariat at HZAU-CHFS.
  • Launch the IPGS digital platform and hold the inaugural meeting.
Phase 2 · 2027

Open membership

  • Open calls for Full and Associate Members.
  • Launch the first four Working Groups.
  • Host the inaugural International Plant Grafting Symposium.
Phase 3 · 2027–28

Research programmes

  • Submit the first joint grant applications.
  • Activate the Doctoral Fellowship programme.
  • Launch the Knowledge Commons and the first Annual State of Grafting Science.
Phase 4 · 2028–30

Consolidation

  • Hold the second International Plant Grafting Symposium.
  • Activate all seven Working Groups at full capacity.
  • Launch the policy-engagement and advocacy programme.
Phase 5 · 2030+

Maturity

  • Self-sustaining funding base.
  • First cohort of IPGS-affiliated doctoral graduates.
  • Established policy influence at FAO, CGIAR, and national levels.
