Establishment Roadmap

From founding to global recognition — a phased, milestone-driven approach spanning 2026–2030+.

Phase 0 · 2026 Q1–Q2

Founding

  • Sign the Charter with Founding Members at the Wuhan Founding Meeting (7–10 May 2026).
  • Appoint a Board at HZAU.
  • Establish the Secretariat at HZAU-CHFS.
Phase 1 · 2026 Q3–Q4

Launch

  • Launch a grafting special issue of Horticulture Advance.
  • Submit the first joint grant applications.
  • Activate the Doctoral Fellowship program.
Phase 2 · 2027

Growth

  • Launch the Knowledge Commons and the first HZAU Summer Exchange Program.
  • Open calls for Full and Associate Members.
  • Activate all seven Working Groups.
Phase 3 · 2027–28

Research & engagement

  • Launch the policy-engagement and technology transfer programs.
  • Explore international joint funding opportunities.
  • Build visibility and engagement with FAO, CGIAR, and national bodies.
Phase 4 · 2028–30

Consolidation

  • Hold the second International Plant Grafting Symposium.
  • Support the first cohort of IPGS-affiliated doctoral graduates.
  • Launch the industry-engagement and technology transfer program.
Phase 5 · 2030+

Maturity

  • Self-sustaining funding base.
  • Established policy influence at FAO, CGIAR, and national levels.
  • Recognized as the global reference society for plant grafting science.
