About IPGS

What is the International Plant Grafting Society?

IPGS is a purely academic international society coordinating research, building shared infrastructure, training researchers, and setting global standards in plant grafting science. The Society is anchored at the College of Horticulture & Forestry Sciences, Huazhong Agricultural University (HZAU), Wuhan, China.

Coordinate Research

Seven thematic Working Groups deliver flagship programs and collaborative projects across member institutions worldwide.

Build Infrastructure

The IPGS Knowledge Commons hosts compatibility data, open protocols, germplasm registries, and FAIR-compliant datasets.

Train Researchers

Joint PhD supervision, postdoctoral mobility, and the HZAU Summer Exchange Programs nurture the next generation.

Set Standards

The Minimum Information for Grafting Experiments (MIGE) and harmonised protocols establish a shared scientific vocabulary.
