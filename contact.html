Join Us

Apply for IPGS membership — full institutional member, associate researcher, or international observer. Review the tiers and benefits below, then submit your application directly to the Secretariat.

Contribution model

Participation, not fees

IPGS membership is open to universities, national agricultural research institutes, and recognized research colleges worldwide. The Society is academic by constitution; commercial entities do not hold membership.

Membership is contribution-based, not fee-based. Members contribute to the Society through:

  • Researcher time in Working Groups and committees.
  • Laboratory, glasshouse, and field-trial capacity.
  • Germplasm and reference datasets deposited in the Knowledge Commons.
  • Co-hosting of workshops, summer schools, and symposia.
  • In-kind secondment of administrative or technical staff to the Secretariat.

A symbolic annual administrative contribution may be introduced from 2028 onward to cover Knowledge Commons hosting costs, subject to ratification by the General Assembly.

The four tiers

How membership is structured

Founding

Senior researchers who attended the 2026 Wuhan Founding Meeting, in person or online.

  • Permanent Board seat
  • IP priority on joint outputs
  • Alliance co-branding rights
  • Charter signatory status

Full

One designated institutional representative per university or national research institute — carries voting rights in the General Assembly.

  • Voting rights in the Assembly
  • Co-PI status on joint projects
  • Full Knowledge Commons access
  • Working-Group leadership eligibility

Associate

Open to individual researchers from any institution worldwide — including those whose institution already holds Full membership.

  • Training-program access
  • Joint-publication eligibility
  • Doctoral Fellowship eligibility
  • Observer rights in the Assembly

Observer

International bodies: FAO, CGIAR, national ministries, and recognized intergovernmental organizations — non-voting with advisory capacity.

  • General Assembly attendance
  • Receipt of IPGS publications
  • Policy-advisory capacity

Application

Apply to join IPGS

Submit your expression of interest using this form. The Secretariat will review your application and respond within five working days. Shortlisted applicants will be asked to provide supporting documents.

What happens next

  1. Secretariat acknowledges your application within 5 working days.
  2. Applications reviewed by the Board in April and October.
  3. Shortlisted applicants provide: institutional profile, letter of support from the Head of Institution, research statement (≤ 1,000 words), and representative's CV.
  4. Board decision communicated within 2 weeks of each review cycle.
