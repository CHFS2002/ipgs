Announcement

IPGS Founding Meeting: Wuhan, 7–10 May 2026

31 March 2026

Invitation letters have been dispatched to the 2026 founding cohort. The meeting will convene senior grafting scientists from Brazil, China, France, Greece, Italy, New Zealand, Spain, and Türkiye at HZAU for the formal signing of the IPGS Charter.

Announcement

IPGS name and identity confirmed

12 February 2026

Following consultation with anchor institutions, the interim Board has confirmed the Society's public identity as the International Plant Grafting Society (IPGS).

Press release

HZAU-CHFS designated host of the IPGS Secretariat

20 January 2026

The College of Horticulture and Forestry Sciences at Huazhong Agricultural University will host the inaugural Secretariat and the Knowledge Commons infrastructure.
