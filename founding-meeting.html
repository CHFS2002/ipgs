7–10 May 2026 · Wuhan, Hubei, China

IPGS Founding Meeting

The inaugural meeting of the International Plant Grafting Society — the moment at which the Charter is signed, the first Board is seated, and the first Working Groups are constituted.

Programme

Four days in Wuhan

DateProgramme
7 May 2026Registration & Welcome Reception
8 May 2026IPGS Founding Meeting — Technical Sessions · Charter signature · Board seating · Working-Group formation
9 May 2026Optional research field and facility visits at HZAU-CHFS and partner sites
10 May 2026Departure
Host & venue

Huazhong Agricultural University

College of Horticulture & Forestry Sciences
No. 1 Shizishan Street · Hongshan District
Wuhan 430070, Hubei Province · China

Wuhan is served by Wuhan Tianhe International Airport (WUH), with direct connections from major East-Asian, European, and Middle-Eastern hubs. Local transfer to the HZAU campus is approximately 45 minutes.

The dedicated Secretariat line for the Founding Meeting will answer all logistics and visa-letter enquiries from invited participants.

Full financial support

HZAU covers travel, accommodation, and meals

For all invited founding participants, HZAU is providing round-trip international airfare, accommodation in Wuhan, and meals during the meeting. A visa-support letter is issued on request.
